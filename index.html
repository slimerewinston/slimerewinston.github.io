📱 iPhone Installation

  1. Long-press the "FCW Mobile" pink button above.
  2. Select Copy from the menu.
  3. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari and choose Add Bookmark.
  4. Tap the X to clear the URL and Paste the code you just copied.
  5. Name it "FCW Mobile" and tap Save.
  6. Visit FC Watch, open your Bookmarks, and tap "FCW Mobile" to launch!

💻 Desktop Installation

  1. Ensure your Bookmarks Bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B).
  2. Drag and drop the pink "FCW Mobile" button directly onto your bar.
  3. Click it while on FC Watch to instantly enable the overhaul.
