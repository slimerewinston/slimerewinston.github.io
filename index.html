FCWatch Mobile v6

📱 iOS Installation

  1. Long press the "FCWatch Mobile v6" button above with one finger and keep holding it.
  2. While still holding, use another finger to tap the Bookmarks icon (📖) at the bottom of Safari.
  3. Drag the link into your Favorites folder and release.
  4. Go to any FC Watch page, tap the Bookmarks icon, and tap "FCWatch Mobile v6" to activate!

💻 Desktop Installation

  1. Show your Bookmarks Bar (Ctrl+Shift+B / Cmd+Shift+B).
  2. Drag the "FCWatch Mobile v6" button above into your Bookmarks Bar.
  3. Visit any FC Watch page and click the bookmark to activate!

The bookmarklet needs to be activated each time you visit FC Watch. The persist module will keep it alive as you navigate between pages.
